What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is the ability to monitor your own and other people's emotions.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ also known as intelligence Quotient indicates a person's intelligence level. People with EQ tend to have better social skills OR what I call it while djing “reading the room”

Why is it important?

Unlike your IQ, which remains stable during your life, emotional intelligence continues to grow and can help understand your everyday learning environments! Self Awareness, Self Regulation and Empathy these are really good tools to have inside and outside workplaces.
